INDIAN FESTIVALS

  • ONAM

    •

    Onam is an annual 10-day harvest festival celebrated by the Malayali community in Kerala, India, during the Malayalam month of Chingam (August-September) to commemorate the homecoming of the mythical King Mahabali.

  • HOLI

    •

    Holi is the Hindu "Festival of Colours," a vibrant, joyful celebration marking the arrival of spring and signifying the victory of good over evil.

  • DIWALI

    •

    Diwali, or "Deepawali," is a five-day Indian festival of lights that symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil.
